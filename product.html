Wild Forest Honey

₹450

100% pure, ethically sourced wild forest honey packed with natural goodness. This honey is harvested from the deep forests of Western Ghats, where bees collect nectar from a variety of wild flowers.

Our honey is minimally processed to retain all its natural enzymes, vitamins, and minerals. It's not just a sweetener, but a nutritional powerhouse that supports immunity and overall wellbeing.

Each jar contains 250g of pure, unadulterated wild honey. No additives, no preservatives, just pure honey as nature intended.

Category: Food & Beverages

Product Details

Our Wild Forest Honey is harvested using sustainable practices that respect both the bees and their natural habitat. The honey is collected from hives located deep within the Western Ghats, away from agricultural lands and pollution.

Features:

  • 100% Pure and Natural
  • Ethically harvested
  • No artificial additives or preservatives
  • Minimally processed to retain nutrients
  • Rich in antioxidants and enzymes
  • Supports local forest-dependent communities

Benefits:

  • Supports immune system health
  • Natural source of energy
  • Soothes sore throats and coughs
  • Has antibacterial and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Contains beneficial antioxidants

250g glass jar with metal lid. Our packaging is plastic-free and fully recyclable.
