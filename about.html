Discover the journey that led to OrganicTrove and our commitment to providing the highest quality organic products while promoting a sustainable lifestyle.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2015, OrganicTrove began with a simple mission: to make organic and sustainable products accessible to everyone. What started as a small venture by two friends passionate about eco-friendly living has now grown into one of India's most trusted organic product retailers.

We believe that every small choice we make has a significant impact on our health and the environment. That's why we're committed to curating products that are not only good for you but also good for the planet.

Our vision is to create a community of conscious consumers who make informed choices about the products they use daily, contributing to a more sustainable future for generations to come.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

At OrganicTrove, sustainability isn't just a buzzwordâ€”it's at the core of everything we do. We carefully select products that are produced using sustainable farming practices, ethical manufacturing processes, and eco-friendly packaging.

Our commitment extends beyond the products we sell. We've implemented numerous initiatives to reduce our carbon footprint:

  • Plastic-free packaging for all our products
  • Carbon-neutral shipping options
  • Solar-powered warehouse and office facilities
  • Zero-waste policy throughout our operations
  • Regular tree planting drives and environmental clean-up initiatives

We're proud to say that we've been recognized for our sustainability efforts with multiple awards, including the "Green Business Award" for three consecutive years.

Supporting Local Communities

We believe in the power of community and the importance of supporting local producers. That's why we work directly with over 50 small-scale farmers, artisans, and producers across India.

By eliminating middlemen, we ensure that our producers receive fair compensation for their work. This not only helps them sustain their livelihoods but also enables them to continue using traditional, sustainable practices that have been passed down through generations.

Through our "Farm to Table" and "Artisan Spotlight" programs, we also help educate consumers about the origins of the products they use and the people behind them, creating a meaningful connection between producers and consumers.
